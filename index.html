Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 31 March 2026

Developer: Kaushal Sadhu

App: TaskFlow Daily

TaskFlow Daily ("the App") is a personal productivity application designed to help you manage tasks and reminders on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally.

All data (tasks, reminders, preferences) is stored locally on your device using local storage.

2. Biometric & Device Authentication

The app may use your device's authentication (fingerprint, face unlock, PIN).

  • No biometric data is stored or accessed
  • Handled entirely by device OS
  • App only gets success/failure result

3. Notifications & Alarms

The app uses local notifications to remind you about tasks.

  • All notifications are generated on-device
  • No data is sent to any server
  • Uses alarm permissions for exact timing

4. Device Permissions

  • Notifications – task reminders
  • Alarm permission – exact timing
  • Boot permission – restore alarms after restart
  • Vibration – notification alerts
  • Biometric – secure access
  • Storage – import/export feature only

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. No external storage or servers are used.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does NOT use analytics, ads, or tracking services in this version.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from users, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in future updates. Changes will be reflected here.

9. Your Rights

  • You can view and edit all your data
  • You can delete tasks anytime
  • You can uninstall app to remove all data
